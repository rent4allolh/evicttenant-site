What is included in the Legal Guarantee

What is included in the Legal Guarantee
  • Complete RTA & LTB Coverage: Comprehensive representation for virtually every scenario under the Residential Tenancies Act.
  • Notice Drafting & Service: Preparation and legal service of all landlord notices, including N4, N5, N7, N8, N12, and N13.
  • Landlord Applications: Preparation, filing, and management of L1, L2, and all other related L-series applications.
  • Tenant Application Defense: Full legal defense and strategic response against tenant-initiated claims, including T-applications (T2, T6, etc.).
  • Hearing Attendance: Full representation by our paralegal team at Landlord and Tenant Board hearings, whether you are the applicant or the respondent.
  • Order Enforcement: Coordination of LTB eviction orders with the local Sheriff's office to regain legal possession.
  • LandlordEzy Membership: Includes secure LTB order uploading and outstanding tenant debt reporting to Equifax.
  • Digital Workflow: Fast onboarding, ongoing file handling, and structured communication from our team.
Important Note: Pricing covers one active matter governed by the Residential Tenancies Act (Ontario). Disbursements (filing fees, sheriff fees) are the responsibility of the owner. Multiple active matters or complex portfolios can be quoted separately.

Frequently asked questions

Clear, straightforward answers to the most common questions landlords have about the LTB process and our digital representation platform.

Do I need to visit an office to start my file?

No. Our entire operation is built for virtual representation. Landlords anywhere in Ontario can book a strategy session, upload documents, and track their LTB file entirely online.

Are government filing fees included?

No. Disbursements, such as LTB filing fees and Sheriff enforcement costs, are the direct responsibility of the property owner and will be pre-approved before proceeding.

What types of matters are included?

We focus heavily on non-payment of rent (N4/L1), conduct and damage disputes (N5/N7), and owner's use recoveries (N12/L2), providing structured representation at the LTB.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. We know property owners manage their portfolios on the go, so our entire intake process and website are fully optimized for smartphones and tablets.
